For adults, a properly maintained mouthguard can typically last 2 to 3 years. To keep it in good condition, rinse it with cold water after use, store it in its original case, and keep it out of direct sunlight. For children and teenagers, mouthguards usually need replacing every year — or even sooner — as their mouths change with growing and shifting teeth. It's a good idea to have your dentist check the fit during regular dental visits.
