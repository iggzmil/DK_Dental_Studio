Mouthguards

Mouthguards help protect your teeth, gums, lips, and jaw from injury during sports or other physical activities. They absorb impact and reduce the risk of broken teeth, jaw injuries, and cuts inside the mouth.

Our Process View

1
First Appointment

Choose your mouthguard colour and have an impression taken of your teeth. Your custom mouthguard will be ready in 2–3 days.

Second Appointment

Come in to collect and try it on. If needed, we'll make quick adjustments on the spot.
