Aftercare

Take your denture out and rinse it under warm water. Use toothbrush with soap or non-abrasive toothpaste to gently clean all surfaces.

At night, soak your denture in water or a denture cleaning solution. Before putting it back in your mouth, rinse it well.

Also, clean your gums and any natural teeth before putting your denture back in.

Important: Never put a dry denture in your mouth always make sure it's wet.

To apply denture adhesive, clean your denture and dry it a tissue. Apply the adhesive in thin strips or dots on the inside of the denture surface. Insert the denture into your mouth, pressing firmly and holding in place for 1 minute to ensure a secure fit.

Note: if you apply too much you will taste it, that means put less next time.

Take your denture out of your mouth. Make yourself a cup of warm water and salt (1 teaspoon is enough). Rinse your mouth with the warm water and salt till the cup is finished. Repeat this twice a day till your blister is gone.

Note: it is important to make sure your denture has been adjusted and fits properly, otherwise the blister will keep returning or it may be its time for a reline.

DK Dental Studio can professionally clean them using a special machine. We recommend this service every 6 months or as needed to keep your dentures fresh, clean, and looking their best.

Keep your dentures either in your mouth or in a denture storage box with water. Never leave them lying around—many people lose their dentures this way, and in some cases, pets (especially dogs) may chew or damage them.
