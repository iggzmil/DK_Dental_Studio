Maintenance & Repairs

Relines

Over time, your gums naturally shrink, but your dentures stay the same. This can make your dentures feel loose, uncomfortable, and harder to eat or speak with. In some cases, it can even be painful.

A denture reline is the solution. We add new material inside your existing denture to fill the gap between your gums and the denture. This gives better support, comfort, and fit restoring how your denture should feel and function. We recommend a reline every 2–3 years to keep your mouth healthy.

Our Process View

1
First Appointment

During your appointment, we'll take an impression of your current denture and begin the reline process.

2
Second Appointment

Leave your denture with us for 1–2 days. When you return, we'll ensure the fit is just right with any final adjustments.

Notice:

If your appointment is in the morning, you can usually collect your denture on the same day. Afternoon appointments may be ready the next day.

Repairs

If your denture is cracked, broken, or a clasp has snapped, we can help. Whether it's a simple or complex issue, we can repair your denture and adjust it to make sure it's comfortable and works properly.

Our Process View

1
First Appointment

During your appointment, we'll take an impression of your current denture and begin the repair process. You will have to leave your denture with us for 1–2 hours.

2
Second Appointment

You will collect your repaired denture and we'll ensure the fit is just right with any final adjustments.

Notice:

If your appointment is in the morning, you can usually collect your denture on the same day. Afternoon appointments may be ready the next day.

Clean and Polish

Over time, dentures can build up stains, plaque, and tartar—even with daily cleaning. At DK Dental Studio, we offer a professional clean and polish service using specialised equipment that gently removes buildup and restores your denture's freshness and shine.

We recommend this service every 6 months or as needed to keep your dentures clean, bright, and feeling comfortable.

Our Process View

1
First Appointment

We'll professionally clean and polish them using our advanced denture-cleaning machine. You will have to leave your denture with us for 2 hours.

2
Second Appointment

Pick up your fresh, clean dentures, ready to wear.

Notice:

This service will make your dentures much cleaner, but they may not look brand new, especially if there is long-term staining or wear.
